The Theory of Doge, often associated with the popular internet meme featuring the Shiba Inu dog breed, explores the concept of humor and virality in online culture. It suggests that absurd or nonsensical content, like the "Doge" meme's broken English phrases, can gain widespread appeal due to its unexpectedness and simplicity, reflecting the unpredictable nature of internet culture. Additionally, the Theory of Doge highlights the role of internet communities in amplifying and perpetuating such memes through sharing and remixing.
